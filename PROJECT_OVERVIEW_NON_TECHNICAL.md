# Non-Technical Project Overview: SAHAHR Website

## 🏠 What is this website?
Think of this website as the **Digital Community Center** for the South Asian Heritage Association of Hamilton & Region (SAHAHR).

Just as a physical community center has a front desk, bulletin boards, and activity rooms, this website serves the same purpose online:
*   **Front Desk**: Tells people who we are (About Us, Mission).
*   **Bulletin Board**: Shows upcoming events and news (Events, Blog).
*   **Resource Center**: Provides help for newcomers, seniors, and youth (Resources).
*   **Direct Line**: Allows people to contact us or sign up for newsletters.

## ⚙️ How does it work? (The Simple Version)
The website is split into two main parts:

### 1. The "Storefront" (What Visitors See)
This is the public website (`sahahr.org`).
*   It is fast, secure, and works on all devices (phones, tablets, laptops).
*   Visitors can browse content, but they cannot change anything here.

### 2. The "Control Panel" (Where You Work)
This is the admin area, also called the **Sanity Studio** (`sahahr.org/studio`).
*   This is a secure area for authorized members only.
*   **You do NOT need to know code to use this.**
*   It works like filling out a form:
    *   Want to add an event? Fill in the "Title", "Date", and "Description" fields.
    *   Want to add a board member? Upload their photo and type their bio.
    *   Click **Publish**, and the website updates automatically!

## 📝 What do I need to do?
As a non-technical member, your role is primarily **Content Manager**.

### Your Responsibilities:
1.  **Keep Events Fresh**:
    *   Log in to the Control Panel once a month.
    *   Add upcoming events so the homepage always looks active.
    *   (Old events are automatically moved to the "Past" section, so you don't need to delete them!)
2.  **Update the Team**:
    *   When the Board of Directors changes, update the photos and names in the Control Panel.
3.  **Share News**:
    *   Use the "Blog" or "News" section to post announcements, success stories, or photos from recent festivals.

## 💰 What does it cost?
The website uses modern technologies that often have generous free tiers for non-profits:
*   **Hosting (Vercel)**: Keeps the website online. Usually free for hobby/non-profit use.
*   **Content System (Sanity)**: Stores your text and images. The free tier is very generous (enough for thousands of visitors).
*   **Domain Name**: The only unavoidable cost (usually ~$15-20/year to keep the `.org` address).

## 🚀 Why is this better than Wix/Wordpress?
*   **Custom Made**: It is built specifically for SAHAHR's needs, not a generic template.
*   **No "Plugin Chaos"**: Unlike WordPress, you don't need to constantly update plugins or worry about the site breaking securely.
*   **Performance**: It loads extremely fast, which is great for people with slower internet connections.

## 📞 Who do I call if it breaks?
*   **Content Issues** (Typos, wrong image): You can fix this yourself in the Control Panel!
*   **Technical Issues** (Site offline, error messages): Contact the technical lead or the developer who handed this over. They can verify the "Hosting" status.

---
*Summary: You own the content. Taking care of the website just means keeping the news and events up to date. The technical machinery runs itself.*
